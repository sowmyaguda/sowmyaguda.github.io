Brain Tumor Detection

The Problem

Detecting tumor regions from MR images quickly and reliably to assist clinicians and enable downstream workflows.

Approach

Experimented with YOLOv7 for localization and a ResNet-based classifier for subtype. Built a preprocessing pipeline with OpenCV and augmentation. Evaluated with cross-validation and ablation.

Tech Used
YOLOv7PyTorchResNetOpenCV
What I Learned

Balancing precision/recall with real constraints, dataset curation matters more than architecture tweaks, and clear instrumentation accelerates iteration.
